About Doctor Batch

Tim Fanelli, a.k.a Doctor Batch, is an IBMer specializing in z Systems workload modernization. His ideas and strategies have driven dozens of Fortune 500 development efforts bringing decades-old mainframe solutions into the 21st century. By exposing traditional MVS developers to modern developer operations, software life cycles, web service architectures, java batch, and business rules management, he is able to transform traditional MVS development paradigms into agile development teams utilizing continuous integration, continuous test, and continuous delivery methodologies; while maintaining compliance with rigid quality assurance and audit requirements imposed on highly regulated industry solutions.

Tim's extensive hands-on experience with IBM customer's has enabled him to achieve recognition as an OpenGroup Master Certified IT Specialist. Tim also serves on the IT Specialist certification board at IBM.

Workload Modernization

CICS, COBOL and PL/I

Tim has deep technical expertise with business rules management, and specializes in IBM's Operational Decision Manager on z/OS for CICS, COBOL and PL/I application modernization.

Java Batch (JSR-352)

Tim served on the expert working group for JSR-352: Batch Applications for the Java Platform, and now specializes in JSR-352 application workloads utilizing WebSphere Liberty Profile on IBM z Systems.

WebSphere Batch

Tim has almost a decade of experience with WebSphere Batch (formerly WebSphere Compute Grid) on z/OS, and now emphasizes application architectures for targeting WebSphere Batch with portability into JSR-352 in Liberty Profile as the technology matures.

Developer Operations

Regardless of infrastructure or middleware, Tim always provides a strong emphasis on adopting best-practices to achieve reduced overhead and decreased time to market with mitigated risk, utilizing continous -test, -integration, and -release methodologies in agile development paradigms.
